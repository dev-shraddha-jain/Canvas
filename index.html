Privacy Policy

for Canvas – a sketching app to draw and download your artwork

Last Updated: August 28, 2025 No account required No artwork upload

Canvas ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application Canvas, which allows you to draw sketches and download your drawings.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information: We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use Canvas.

Drawings & Sketches: Your artwork stays on your device. We do not collect, store, or have access to your drawings.

App Usage Data (optional): We may collect anonymous diagnostics (e.g., crash reports, performance metrics) to improve the app. This data does not identify you.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide core drawing and download functionality;
  • Improve performance and fix issues (if diagnostics are enabled);
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers.

3. Storage of Your Data

All sketches and downloads are stored locally on your device. We do not upload or back up your drawings to our servers.

4. Third‑Party Services

Canvas may use trusted third-party services (e.g., crash reporting or analytics) to collect non-personal, anonymous statistics such as device model, OS version, and crash logs. For specific details, review the policies of any services you enable in the app (e.g., Firebase Crashlytics/Analytics).

5. Your Privacy Choices

  • Delete your sketches directly from your device at any time.
  • Opt out of analytics/diagnostics where supported by your device or within the app settings.

6. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Because no personal data is required to use Canvas, the app is suitable for all ages.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be indicated by the Last Updated date at the top of this page.
